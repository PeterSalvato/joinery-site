Research

Before any of this was a school, it was a practice. These six papers document three years of applied work — what the methodology is, where it comes from, and why it works.

The courses translate each paper into hands-on work on your own project. The papers are the foundation. The courses are the application. Read the papers to understand the thinking. Take the courses to apply it to your practice.

All six papers are open access. The tools are open source. The site you are reading was built by the system it teaches.
