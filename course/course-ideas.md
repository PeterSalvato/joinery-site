# Course Ideas (Captured 2026-04-04)

These are raw ideas. Not scoped, not designed. Captured for future development.

## Domain-Specific Foundations Variants

The Foundations course is designed for designers. The same three-domain structure (task decomposition, input inversion, voice governance) applies to other creative disciplines with different examples and exercises.

- **Foundations for Authors** -- novel/fiction writers integrating AI into their writing practice. Decomposition applied to story structure. Input inversion applied to brainstorming and drafting. Voice governance applied to maintaining authorial voice.
- **Foundations for Copywriters/Marketers** -- content professionals using AI for brand copy, campaigns, content strategy. Decomposition applied to brief-to-deliverable workflows. Input inversion applied to creative concepting. Voice governance applied to brand voice at scale.
- **Foundations for Designers (Visual Tools)** -- designers using AI as the new production tools (image generation, ComfyUI, Midjourney, etc. as the new ImageMagick/Photoshop). Decomposition applied to multi-step generation workflows. Input inversion applied to reference and mood direction. Voice governance applied to visual consistency.

## Notes

- Examples and exercises change per audience
