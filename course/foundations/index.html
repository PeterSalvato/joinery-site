Course 01 · Designer Track

AI Accommodation: Foundations for Designers

3 modules · 18 lessons · Self-paced · Applied to your own work

Designers who use AI lose authorship. This course teaches you to use the attunement skill you already have to stay in the author's seat.

You already attune to clients, audiences, customers every day. Reading what they need and designing around that reading is how you do your work. This course applies that same attunement skill to AI as a new receiving system.

Three modules. Each one teaches a different aspect of the methodology applied to your own real project. You finish with a working system for your practice, deliverables you use immediately, and a clear path to the deeper practitioner courses.

Your Modules

Module 1 · 6 lessons

Task Decomposition

Attunement to the AI's task-processing limits. Why compound prompts fail the same way compound instructions fail in a classroom. The three-way comparison: compound, harnessed, and human-CD synthesis. You step through specialist outputs, make tracked decisions, and compile the creative direction brief.
Module 2 · 6 lessons

Input Inversion

Attunement to the AI's input capacity. Structured prompts cap your output at the level of the prompt. Unstructured human thinking (brain dumps, voice memos, working notes) carries richer raw material. You build an input practice on your own project and see what changes when the AI receives your actual thinking instead of your pre-organized conclusions.
Module 3 · 6 lessons

Voice Governance

Attunement to the AI's generation defaults. AI copy sounds the same because voice constraints are applied after generation, not during. You extract how you actually talk from your own conversations, codify it as generation rules, and apply those rules before the first word is produced. The output sounds like you because the constraints occupied the space where the AI's defaults would have gone.

What Comes After Foundations

Foundations introduces three domains at entry level. Each domain has its own practitioner course that goes deep: Voice Governance, Input Inversion, Lens Extraction, Semantic Hierarchy, Prosthetic Cognition, and Coordinator Building. Foundations gives you the first move in each domain. The practitioner courses give you the full practice.
