About Joinery

Joinery teaches one skill applied to a new medium: attunement. Reading what a receiving system actually needs, then designing around that reading. Designers already do this with clients and audiences. Joinery teaches you to do it with AI.

The methodology comes from special education pedagogy and federal disability law (IDEA). It is grounded in published cognitive science research and built by a trained educator.

Peter Salvato

Joinery was built by a designer who studied graphic design and printmaking at SVA (under James Victore), studied education at Brooklyn College, taught special education in Brooklyn public schools, and taught visual studies and intro to graphic design at Kingsborough Community College, before spending twelve years as a product design lead. The methodology comes from the classroom, where designing around the processing reality of the receiving system is federal law. The attunement skill underneath everything Peter does, reading what a student, a client, an AI actually needs before designing for it, was learned in a self-contained special education classroom in Sunset Park.

Accommodation Design is not a theory. It is a practice developed across 25 years of making things in rooms where the gap between intent and execution was the whole problem: classrooms, print shops, construction sites, recording studios, enterprise platforms, and AI systems.

Design Education School of Visual Arts (graphic design, printmaking, screen printing under James Victore). Brooklyn College (M.A. program in education). Kingsborough Community College (taught intro to graphic design and visual studies). NYC Department of Education (special education, self-contained classroom, Sunset Park).
Design Practice 25 years across graphic design, print production, brand systems, and product design. 12 years as product design lead in enterprise software. Brand systems for independent businesses. The methodology comes from design practice, not from engineering.
Research Six published whitepapers with DOIs. Accommodation Design, Input Inversion, Prosthetic Cognition, Semantic Hierarchy, Lens Extraction, Voice Governance. Citations include Sweller, Cowan, Murdock, Liu et al., Bock, Pennebaker, Flower & Hayes, Bereiter & Scardamalia, Wong et al.
Infrastructure 130+ working AI skills, agents, and voice protocols in daily production use. The tooling the methodology describes is the tooling the founder uses.
