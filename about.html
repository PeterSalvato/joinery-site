The Founder

Peter Salvato

Joinery was built by a designer who spent five years teaching special education in Brooklyn public schools before spending twelve years as a product design lead in enterprise software. The methodology comes from the classroom. The tools were built and tested in production. The research is published.

Accommodation Design is not a theory. It is a practice developed across 25 years of making things in rooms where the gap between intent and execution was the whole problem: classrooms, print shops, construction sites, recording studios, enterprise platforms, and AI systems.

Education NYC Department of Education, Brooklyn College, Kingsborough Community College. Self-contained special education, design history, visual studies.
Design 12 years product design lead. Enterprise platform architecture, design systems, AI strategy. Brand systems for independent businesses.
Research Six published whitepapers with DOIs. Accommodation Design, Input Inversion, Prosthetic Cognition, Semantic Hierarchy, Lens Extraction, Voice Governance.
Infrastructure 130+ working AI skills, agents, and voice protocols in daily production use. The tooling the methodology describes is the tooling the founder uses.
