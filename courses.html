Curriculum

Six courses. Your own real project. Each one builds on the last.

Every course is grounded in a published research paper and taught using a specific pedagogical method chosen for what that course teaches. You start with Foundations, which gives you three structural moves you can apply the same day. Each course after that takes one domain deeper. By the end of the ladder you have a complete methodology: input, voice, evaluation, context, and coordination, all wired into your own practice. After the ladder, specialist tracks apply the full system to specific creative disciplines.
