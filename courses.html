Curriculum

Joinery teaches six sequential courses, each grounded in published research and taught with named pedagogy. You work on your own real project throughout. The courses build on each other: each one assumes you have the practices from the one before it. After the full ladder, specialist tracks apply the complete methodology to specific creative practices.
