Joinery Systemworks

AI methodology for creative practitioners

Creatives face two AI fears at once. Don't use it, get replaced. Use it, and everything you make looks like everything everyone else is making.

Both fears point at the same gap: there is no framework for using AI that keeps the work distinctly yours.

The Methodology

Accommodation Design

A structural methodology for integrating AI into creative practice without losing the thing that makes your work valuable: you.

Not prompt tutorials. Not tool roundups. A framework for building AI workflows where your judgment stays in the room and your output stays yours.
