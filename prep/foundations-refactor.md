## 2. The Three-Week System

### Week 1: The Directorial Scaffold (Task Decomposition)
- **Problem:** A 1,000-word compound prompt is a liability. Its attention splits, and the output defaults to the mean.
- **The Move:** Decompose your vision into a squad of independent specialists. Each specialist provides the **Deep Raw Material** for one dimension of the work.
- **The Multiplier:** We show you how to build the **Directorial Scaffold (The Interviewer)**—an AI coordination layer that interviews *you* on the specialist reports, extracting your judgment to synthesize the output in minutes.
- **Outcome:** A directorial system for one real project. 

### Week 2: System Inversion (Input Inversion)
- **Problem:** Structured prompts compress what makes your thinking yours. 
- **The Move:** Flip the burden of organization. We teach you **Input Inversion**—an ingestion practice where you talk, brainstorm, and argue with yourself, and let the system find the architecture. 
- **Outcome:** An input practice that feeds your raw soul into the machine's engine.

### Week 3: Operational Governance (Voice Governance)
- **Problem:** AI copy sounds the same because voice rules are applied *after* generation, not during. 
- **The Move:** We teach you to extract your unique speech patterns from conversation and codify them as **Operational Rules.** These rules occupy the space where the AI's defaults would have gone.
- **Outcome:** A voice protocol that protects your identity at the source.
