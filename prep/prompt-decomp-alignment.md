## 2. The Refined Alignment (The "How Peter Works")
*Decomposition is not a clerical task of splitting text. It is a strategic act of **Isolation for Evaluation**.*

### Key Shift: From "Splitting" to "Specializing"
In your actual work, you don't just split a prompt; you create **Specialists**.
- **Compound:** "Write a brand brief for a hot sauce company." (The AI guesses).
- **Decomposed (Joinery):** 
    - Specialist A: "Analyze the competitive landscape of 'craft' vs 'mass-market' spicy condiments."
    - Specialist B: "Extract the core emotional resonance of 'Roughing It' (vulnerability + grit)."
    - Specialist C: "Define the visual vocabulary (woodblock print, distressed textures, limited palette)."

### The "Synthesis" Reality
The site implies synthesis is the final step. In your work, **Synthesis is the ONLY step that matters.**
- The AI's job is to provide the **Deep Raw Material**.
- Your job (as the CD) is to **Synthesize the Disconnects**. 
- *Example:* "Specialist A says we are craft, but Specialist C says the visuals feel mass-market. I am choosing to lean into the craft tension." **The AI cannot do this.**
