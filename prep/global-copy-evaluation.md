# Global Copy Evaluation: Joinery Site Refactor

**Status:** STAGED / PREP
**Objective:** Identify the "Drift" between current copy and the refined "Master Creative / Creative Systems" positioning.

---

## 1. Homepage (`index.html`)

### A. The Hero Section
- **Current:** "A school for creative practitioners who use AI."
- **The Drift:** Too passive. Sounds like a club, not an institution.
- **The Fix:** Lead with **Authorship and Force Multiplication.** "The architecture of creative authorship. We teach the structural methodology for scaling vision through modern tooling."

### B. The Problem Section
- **Current:** "Creatives face two fears... don't use AI get replaced... use it lose distinctiveness."
- **The Drift:** Frames AI as a threat to be managed (Fear-based).
- **The Fix:** Frame AI as an **Engine to be Directed** (Leverage-based). "The tools are scaling; the authorship is failing. Joinery is the first school to solve the directorial problem the labs are ignoring."

### C. The Methodology Section
- **Current:** "Accommodation Design... grounded in special education pedagogy... built by a trained educator."
- **The Drift:** Frames your background as a "credential."
- **The Fix:** Frame it as a **Structural Mechanic.** "We use the rigorous mechanics of special education to build a **Directorial Scaffold**—ensuring your creative intent survives the 10x multiplier of the AI."

---

## 2. Foundations Sales Page (`courses/foundations.html`)

### A. The Value Prop
- **Current:** "Designers who use AI lose authorship... stay in the author's seat."
- **The Drift:** Defensive. "Staying in the seat" is the bare minimum.
- **The Fix:** Offensive. **"Multiply your directorial bandwidth."** Sell the ability to do 20 hours of CD-level strategy in 50 minutes.

### B. Week 1: Task Decomposition
- **Current:** "You learn to decompose, run independently, and synthesize."
- **The Drift:** Sounds like more clerical work (splitting prompts).
- **The Fix:** **"The Squad of Specialists."** "Learn to run a room of AI specialists. We show you how to build the **Coordination Layer** that interviews you, extracting your judgment to synthesize the output in minutes."

### C. The Artifact (The "Hot Sauce" Demo)
- **Current:** Text comparison of a logo description.
- **The Drift:** "Prompt Engineering 101."
- **The Fix:** **"Directorial Synthesis."** The artifact must show the **Peter-level override.** Show the specialist reports, show the "Interviewer" transcript, and show the **Master Brief** the AI could not have written alone.

---

## 3. Research Page (`research.html`)

### A. The IA
- **Current:** A flat list of DOI links.
- **The Drift:** "Cold" library feel. 
- **The Fix:** **"The Evidence Base."** Frame the research as the **Source Code of the Methodology.** Anchor every whitepaper in a real-world "Modern Tooling" problem. 

### B. The Narrative
- **Current:** "Read the papers to understand the thinking."
- **The Drift:** Passive.
- **The Fix:** **"The Category Creation Move."** "Joinery is the first out of the gate with this research. We are proposing the new standard for creative systems to the field."

---

## 4. About Page (`about.html`)

### A. The Lineage
- **Current:** Chronological resume (SVA, DOE, Enterprise).
- **The Drift:** Individual history.
- **The Fix:** **"Lineage of Mastery."** "Victore taught me the 'Who.' Special education taught me the 'How.' 25 years of practice taught me the 'System.' Joinery is the convergence."

---

## Summary of Today's "Pass":
We are moving the entire site from **"AI Coaching for Designers"** to **"The Institution for Creative Systems Engineering."** 

We are replacing "Prompting" with **"Directing,"** and "Automation" with **"Force Multiplication."**

**Which section do you want to refactor first?** I propose the **Homepage Hero and Manifesto** to set the tone for everything else.
