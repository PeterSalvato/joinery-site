# Positioning: Harness Engineering vs. Joinery Decomposition

**Status:** STAGED / PREP
**Objective:** Position Joinery as the human-centered, creative-first alternative to the technical "Harness Engineering" movement.

## The "Third Evolution" (The Shared Ground)
The industry is moving past "Prompt Engineering" (fiddling with words) and into "Harness Engineering" (designing the environment). Both Joinery and Anthropic agree: **The structure of the interaction matters more than the words in the prompt.**

## The Joinery Edge (Your "Weaponized" Message)

### 1. The "Special Ed" Advantage
Anthropic "discovered" that models need harnesses to function. Peter Salvato has known this for 20 years because he taught in a **special education** classroom. Harnesses aren't a new AI trick; they are **accommodations**. Joinery is the only school teaching you how to apply 20 years of pedagogical rigor to your creative workflow.

### 2. The Synthesis Ceiling
Harness engineering is built for things that have a "Correct" answer (code, math, data). It uses "Inviolable Tests." **Creative work has no unit test.** If you let an AI "Evaluator" judge your brand strategy, you get a "competent" brand that looks like every other brand. Joinery teaches you how to decompose so you can step into the gap and apply **Judgment** where the harness only applies **Logic**.

### 3. Isolation for Tension, not for Cleanup
A technical harness wants to remove friction. A Joinery decomposition wants to **reveal tension**. By isolating "Strategy" from "Visuals," you can see where they contradict each other. That contradiction is where the "New" happens. A purely automated harness would "fix" that tension into a generic consensus.
