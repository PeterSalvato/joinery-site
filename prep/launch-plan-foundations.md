## 2. The "20-10-1" Daily Distribution Cadence
Since you have no audience, you must go to theirs.

- Target: 5-10 specific designers/CDs (Victore's orbit, Draplin, etc.).
- Goal: Do not pitch. **Attune** to their post. If they complain about AI "slop," provide a structural reason why (e.g., "It's a decomposition failure, not a model failure").
- Outcome: Profile views → Link in bio → Sales page.

### 10 Minutes: Reddit/Community Scan
- r/graphic_design, r/web_design, r/freelanceWriters.
- Search for: "AI taking jobs," "prompts," "ChatGPT sucks for design."
- Action: Provide the "Roughing It" 3-way comparison as a comment/solution.
